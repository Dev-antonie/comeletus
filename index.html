About Us

Come Let Us exists to promote social inclusion for the public benefit by preventing BME people from becoming socially excluded, relieving the needs of those people who are emotionally and socially excluded and assisting them to integrate into society.

Need Help? Get In Touch

Our Vision

To promote social inclusion for public benefit of BME people, who are emotionally excluded from society.

  • Create opportunity to explore new learning
  • Increased self confidence and the ability to make more informed choices
  • Improve IT based learning activities
  • Improve social networks and activities

Our Mission

We provide facilities to enable BME people to integrate in society.

The Project

Harmony Echoes

Project AIMS to address gaps in bereavement care of BME young people by taking a preventative approach to ensuring that Coronavirus bereaved young people will have support they need at the time they need it, to stay active, engaged and involved with their communities.

Project offers:

  • Recruitment and training of bereavement supporters
  • A dedicated website, social platforms and leaflets to reach those in need
  • Food parcels, hot meals, and a freephone helpline
  • Bereavement virtual counselling and family mental health awareness sessions
